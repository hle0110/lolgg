Credits and APIs Used

lolgg

Data

Schedule, scores, standings, and live in-game stats all come from lolesports.com's public API, the same data behind the official LoL Esports site. Only matches and tournaments from 2023 onward are loaded, to keep the site fast.

When a tournament's official bracket isn't published yet, this site pulls a fallback bracket from Liquipedia so the page isn't left blank.

Icons

Riot's Data Dragon for champion and item icons; Community Dragon for objective icons.

Streams

Official Twitch and YouTube embeds only, loaded once you click play. decapi.me checks whether an official channel or a co-streamer is actually live before showing their embed.

Not shown here

Prize pools, full rosters, drafts, and deep player stats have no reliable public source for a static site, so those pages link out to Liquipedia instead.

Contact form

The contact form is delivered through Web3Forms, a third-party form relay, so this site's inbox address isn't exposed in the page.
